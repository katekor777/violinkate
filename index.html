Hi, I'm Kate! I'm a professionally trained violinist and electric LED performer with 11 years of professional experience. Based in Milan, Italy, I've brought vibrant energy and elegance to stages in 38 countries from fashion shows and luxury weddings to clubs, theatres, and cruise ships. My performances blend style, grace, and a powerful stage presence, turning music into an atmosphere of beauty and emotion.

As an Electric LED Violinist, I don't stay rooted to one spot I move effortlessly through the crowd, weaving the music into the room itself. That mobility, paired with my signature LED violin (which audiences love and photographers adore), makes each show immersive and unforgettable.

Whether in Italy, across Europe, or worldwide, I tailor every set to the moment elevating weddings, corporate events, and private celebrations with polished, high-impact music that feels alive.
